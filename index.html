Baringo countyStudents Association (BUSCA) is a registered, student-led organization uniting learners from Baringo County across public and private universities, colleges, and TVET institutions in Kenya. Founded in 2022, BUSCA champions the welfare and development of students by providing a structured platform for engagement, representation, and support. We uphold values of integrity, inclusivity, and leadership, serving as a collective voice for students in academic, social, and county matters.

BUSCA operates as a transformative platform where students can thrive through leadership training, career development, and active civic engagement. Our programs include mentorship initiatives, annual general meetings, cultural showcases, county development forums, and advocacy on education policies. By fostering partnerships with the county government, NGOs, and institutions, we create opportunities that empower students to contribute meaningfully to their communities and beyond.

Our Mission

To unite, empower, and represent students from Baringo County by fostering academic excellence, leadership development, cultural identity, and strategic partnerships. BUSCA is committed to addressing common student challenges, advocating for student welfare, and serving as a bridge between learners and key stakeholders, including government and development partners.

Our Services

Scholarship Advocacy

We actively lobby for increased bursary allocations and fair distribution processes to ensure that deserving students from Baringo County receive the financial support they need to pursue higher education.

Student Support & Mentorship

BUSCA supports current students through mentorship programs, peer networks, and outreach initiatives that address academic, social, and personal development needs.

Institutional Engagement

We collaborate with universities, colleges, and TVET institutions to represent student interests, enhance communication, and improve the student experience across institutions.

Community Empowerment

By organizing cultural events, youth forums, and community outreach activities, BUSCA strengthens ties between students and the county, fostering pride, leadership, and collective progress.
